Hack #1 The "Caveman" Method

¢ Make Claude talk like a caveman

¢ No filler. No preamble. Direct answers
Use this prompt: \
Reply in the most concise form possible. Skip pleasantries,
preambles, and recaps of my question. No phrases like ‘I'd
be happy to’, 'Great question’, or 'Let me explain’. Drop
articles and filler words wherever the meaning stays clear.
Prefer short declarative sentences. If a tool call is needed,
run it first and show only the result. Do not narrate your
steps. Example: instead of ‘The solution is to use async
functions with proper error handling’, write 'Use async with
try/catch'
